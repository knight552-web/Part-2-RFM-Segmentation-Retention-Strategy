# Retention Strategy Report

## Budget Prioritization
1. At Risk Customers
2. High-Value Unhappy Customers
3. Loyal Customers
4. New Customers
5. Browsers Not Buyers
6. Dormant Customers

## Segment Recommendations

### Champions
VIP rewards, referrals, early-access launches.

### Loyal Customers
Personalized recommendations and loyalty acceleration.

### At Risk Customers
Win-back campaigns, reminder emails, limited-time offers.

### Dormant Customers
Low-cost automated reactivation campaigns.

### Discount Sensitive Customers
Bundle offers and controlled discounting.

### High-Value Unhappy Customers
Priority support, recovery outreach, personalized retention.

### Browsers Not Buyers
Cart recovery and checkout optimization.

### New Customers
Onboarding and first-repeat-purchase incentives.

## Key Insight
Inactivity and disengagement are the strongest indicators of churn risk, while dissatisfaction signals identify high-value customers requiring proactive retention.
